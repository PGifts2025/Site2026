Circular PAC X/Y convention check

Two interpretations of the (X, Y) coordinates:
Amber (dashed): X/Y is TOP-LEFT of the bounding box. Circle centre = (X + D/2, Y + D/2).
Green (dashed): X/Y IS the circle centre. Circle centre = (X, Y).
Whichever circle aligns with the visible engraving/print zone tells us the convention.
